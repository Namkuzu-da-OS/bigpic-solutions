## PART 3: STARLINK DEEP DIVE

### Why Starlink IS the IPO

Starlink generated 69% of SpaceX revenue in 2025 and will generate ~79% in 2026. Revenue is growing at 80% YoY vs. only 9% for launch services. Essentially all of SpaceX's growth comes from Starlink. Investors are buying a rapidly scaling satellite ISP that happens to also launch rockets.

### Satellite Constellation
- **Active satellites:** 9,400+ in LEO (largest constellation in history)
- **V2 Mini (current):** ~100 Gbps per satellite
- **V3 (launching 2026 via Starship):** 1+ Tbps downlink per satellite (10x improvement). 60 satellites per Starship launch, adding 60 Tbps per flight
- **Approved constellation:** Up to 34,400 satellites
- **Laser inter-satellite links:** Create orbital mesh network, reduce ground station dependency

### Direct-to-Cell (Game Changer)
- **Launched:** July 23, 2025 with T-Mobile (T-Satellite)
- **Current:** SMS/MMS and limited data across Continental US, PR, Hawaii, Alaska
- **Satellites:** 650+ DTC satellites launched
- **Device compatibility:** ~60 phone models (Samsung, Motorola, iPhone) -- no special hardware needed
- **Future:** V3 DTC satellites to deliver full 5G cellular from space
- **Significance:** Transforms Starlink from a dish-based service to a ubiquitous cellular supplement reaching every smartphone

### Starshield (Military Starlink)
- NRO: $1.8B classified spy satellite constellation (150+ satellites launched)
- MILNET: 480+ dedicated military LEO satellites, $13B potential program value
- Space Force BADASS: $1B ordering agreement through 2027
- First military satellites deploying mid-2026

### Competitive Moat
- **Amazon Kuiper (Leo):** 180 satellites launched vs. Starlink's 9,400+. Enterprise preview Nov 2025. Full commercial launch Q1 2026. Years behind but backed by $10B+ Amazon commitment and ecosystem bundling (Prime, AWS)
- **OneWeb (Eutelsat):** 600 satellites, $187M revenue. Enterprise/government focus only.
- **Telesat Lightspeed:** 198 planned satellites. Not launching until late 2026.
- **AST SpaceMobile:** Direct-to-cell competitor with AT&T/Verizon partnerships.

### Total Addressable Market
- Satellite internet market: $14.56B (2025) to $33.44B by 2030
- Unserved population: 2.6-3B people worldwide ($74B untapped opportunity)
- Maritime: $6.25B+ by 2030
- When including direct-to-cell (every smartphone in coverage gaps), the true TAM is multiples of the satellite internet figure
